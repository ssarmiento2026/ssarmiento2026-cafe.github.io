About

The Viennese Cafe

The Viennese Cafe is a classical Music cafe catering to classical music lovers who want to enjoy the luxury of eating European desserts in a tranquil environment whilst enjoying Strauss waltzes or Mozart Concertos in the background.

The eponymous "Viennese Cafe" is inspired by Vienna, Austria, the "music capital" of said country. Most Austrian composers go there to start their career as composers. Knowing that Austrian desserts aren't so ubiquitous, the owner, who loves baking, has made it her mission to spread the popularity of Austrian pastries outside of Europe.

Eating here will most likely make you feel like a noble from the 18th century, so why not pay us a visit, hm? We open from 9:00 a.m. to 9:00 p.m. everyday to give you the best experience for the whole day!
