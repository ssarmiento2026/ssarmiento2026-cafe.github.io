Welcome to The Viennese Cafe!

An Austrian Classical Music Cafe that is here to serve you authentic Austrian pastries while you bask in the mellifluous sounds of classical works by famous Austrian composers

Food

The Viennese Cafe has a lot of dessert selections ranging from crepe-like desserts to Apricot cakes. The choices aren't only limited to Austrian desserts, as we have French desserts and beverages too. To check for what's in store, browse through our menu!
